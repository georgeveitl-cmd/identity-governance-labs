# Access Review Process

## Overview

Access reviews ensure users retain only the permissions required for their job responsibilities. Regular reviews reduce risk from excessive privileges and orphaned accounts.

## Review Frequency

Recommended review intervals:

• Privileged roles: Monthly  
• Standard roles: Quarterly  
• Sensitive systems: Quarterly  

## Access Review Steps

1. Export current role assignments
2. Identify privileged access
3. Verify business justification
4. Remove unnecessary permissions
5. Document review results

## Common Findings

• Users with outdated access
• Privileged roles assigned to non-administrators
• Service accounts with excessive permissions
